# Privacy Policy of Photos

This app itself does not collect any personal information of any kind. It does however provide the option to sign in to Google Drive, OneDrive and PCloud, in which case their privacy policies apply:

- [Google Drive's privacy policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- [OneDrive's privacy policy](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/sharepoint/onedrive-privacy-security-overview)
- [PCloud's privacy policy](https://www.pcloud.com/privacy_policy)
